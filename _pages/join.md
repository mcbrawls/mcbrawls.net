MC Brawls is free to play on **Minecraft: Java Edition**.

## How to Connect

1. Click **"Multiplayer"**
2. Click **"Add Server"**
3. Type `play.mcbrawls.net` into the **"Server Address"** field.
4. Set **"Server Resource Packs"** to **"Enabled"**.
5. Click done, and join MC Brawls!
