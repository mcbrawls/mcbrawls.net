## Changes

- Added Privacy GUI to modify your privacy settings (`/privacy`)
    - Message Privacy (Choose who can send you private messages)
    - Party Invite Privacy (Choose who can send you party invites)
    - Activity Display Privacy (Choose who can see your status)
    - Statistics Display Privacy (Choose who can see statistics on your profile)
